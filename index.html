About Republic Day

Republic Day is celebrated on 26 January every year to honor the day when the Constitution of India came into effect in 1950. It symbolizes justice, liberty, equality, and fraternity.

Let us remember the sacrifices of our freedom fighters and pledge to build a strong and united India.
